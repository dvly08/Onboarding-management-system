New CTR On-Boarding Manager Checklist (v2)
==========================================

Use this checklist for every new Support Contractor. It mirrors the contractor onboarding guide and calls out exactly which apps to request and when.

Phase 1: Pre‑Boarding (Before First Week)
-----------------------------------------

Access and invites:

- [ ] Add contractor to EDU/Support Weekly Huddle invite.
- [ ] Add contractor to EDU All Hands (ping Molly McKnight or current owner).
- [ ] Ensure contractor is added to any relevant org-wide or team calendars.

Communication:

- [ ] Send welcome email (use standard template and link to onboarding doc).
- [ ] Confirm start date, start time, and time zone.

Training assignments:

- [ ] Assign initial Lessonly courses:
  - Orientation and basic Student/Employer modules.
  - Any required security/compliance courses for contractors.

Support structure:

- [ ] Assign an onboarding buddy (Support CTR buddy).
- [ ] Schedule at least two buddy meetings in the first two weeks.
- [ ] Clarify buddy expectations (questions, guidance, feedback).

App requests (pre‑boarding bundle):

- [ ] Confirm IT/ops requested: Workday, Slack, Google Workspace, Okta baseline apps.

Phase 2: Start of First Week (Day 1–2)
--------------------------------------

Slack and collaboration:

- [ ] Add contractor to Slack channels:
  - `temp-support-ctr-onboarding`
  - Core team channels
  - Help channels (#help-*, #support-urgent, etc. as appropriate)
- [ ] Post a short intro in the main team channel.

App requests (Week 1 bundle):

- [ ] Request Prod/Demo Admin accounts in Lumos:
  - All 4 account types in both prod and demo (Admin, Student, Employer, CS).
  - Approve the Lumos request when email arrives (manager approval required).
- [ ] Add contractor to Support-T&S Enablement team in Linear.
- [ ] Request Looker access in Lumos (if your current policy allows at this stage).
- [ ] Request Mailtrap access using the internal link.
- [ ] Request FullStory access in Linear.

1:1 framework:

- [ ] Create a recurring 1:1 meeting on calendar.
- [ ] Create a 1:1 doc (notes, agenda, feedback).

Phase 3: End of Week 1
----------------------

Validation:

- [ ] Confirm contractor has all 4 account types in both prod and demo.
- [ ] Check Lessonly completion for Week 1 orientation modules.
- [ ] Confirm they attended:
  - EDU Weekly Huddle (or document why not).
  - “Welcome to Global Support: CTR Orientation.”
  - At least one session with onboarding buddy.

Follow‑up:

- [ ] Ask about any access gaps or blockers.
- [ ] Align on Week 2 focus (Student/Employer vs EDU ramp).

Phase 4: Ticket Review (Weeks 2–5, EDU Onboarding)
---------------------------------------------------

Training and app requests (EDU bundle):

- [ ] Assign EDU Lessonly courses:
  - Intro to EDU and Success.
  - EDU Product modules.
  - EDU Custom Reports.
- [ ] Request Zendesk access in Linear (if not already provisioned).
- [ ] Add contractor to EDU L1 group in Linear.
  - Set starting Open Limit = 3 tickets.
- [ ] Request Playvox access in Lumos.
- [ ] Submit Ops Linear ticket as needed to complete Playvox setup.

Shadowing structure:

- [ ] Ask EDU seniors to schedule at least one shadow session each for Week 2 and Week 3:
  - Mix of Student/Employer and EDU-focused tickets as appropriate.
- [ ] Ensure both:
  - Classic shadowing (contractor watches).
  - Reverse shadowing (contractor shares screen, senior observes).

Monitoring and coaching:

- [ ] Regularly review contractor’s tickets during ticket review:
  - AHT trends vs Month 1 baseline.
  - QA score and feedback themes.
  - Correct use of macros, SOPs, internal notes, and escalation paths.
- [ ] Encourage use of AAQ and office hours.
- [ ] Adjust ticket limits as quality and confidence increase.

Phase 5: Phone Onboarding (Weeks 6–8)
-------------------------------------

Setup (phone bundle):

- [ ] Assign a phone onboarding partner.
- [ ] Add contractor to EDU Phone Group in Linear.
- [ ] Configure phone permissions:
  - Listen to calls.
  - Place/receive calls.
  - Combined permissions group in Linear if used.

Schedule:

- [ ] Week 1 (phones):
  - First three shifts: contractor listens to calls with buddy.
  - Last two shifts: contractor calls back voicemails with buddy listening.
- [ ] Week 2 (phones):
  - Reverse-shadow shifts: contractor takes most calls, buddy observes.
  - Contractor takes scheduled Zoom calls with buddy available to support.
- [ ] Add contractor to phone schedule (not alone initially).

Coaching and metrics:

- [ ] Reinforce phone goals:
  - 100% CSAT on calls.
  - 0 missed or declined calls (unless documented extenuating circumstances).
- [ ] Reinforce phone etiquette:
  - Start of shift: update #support-urgent status and clear previous name.
  - During shift: mark calls/VMs taken with a check or approved reaction.
  - End of shift: scroll back and ensure no posts went unanswered; stop taking new calls ~10 minutes before end of day.

Phase 6: Finish Onboarding (1–2 Weeks After Phone Onboarding)
-------------------------------------------------------------

Scaling up:

- [ ] Increase Open Limit to 10 in Linear (or your current standard for fully ramped EDU L1).
- [ ] Add contractor to Support On‑Call Coverage rotation.

Performance review:

- [ ] Review performance vs Month 1–3 metrics:
  - Ticket metrics (AHT, CSAT, volume, SLAs).
  - QA scores (trend and consistency).
  - Training completion (Lessonly and live trainings).
- [ ] Gather feedback from:
  - Onboarding buddy.
  - Seniors who ran shadow sessions.
  - QA reviewers.

Wrap‑up:

- [ ] Hold formal onboarding wrap‑up meeting:
  - Share feedback and wins.
  - Clarify ongoing expectations beyond onboarding.
  - Discuss growth areas and potential specializations (Student, Employer, EDU, phones, projects).

Phase 7: After Onboarding (Ramp & Conversion Readiness)
-------------------------------------------------------

Access:

- [ ] Provide Looker access once they are at L2 (as policy allows) and have completed required training.

Conversion readiness:

- [ ] Track contractor progress vs conversion criteria:
  - Quantitative: AHT, CSAT, volume, QA, SLAs, training completion.
  - Qualitative: communication, ownership, collaboration, values alignment.
  - SOP and code-of-conduct adherence.
- [ ] When relevant headcount opens, provide input to Support/T&S leadership on readiness for FTE conversion and document rationale.

Global Reminders for Managers
-----------------------------

- Technical:
  - [ ] Reinforce that contractors must not test in production or use the word “test” in visible data.
  - [ ] Reinforce: no posting on product before office hours/AAQ when in doubt.

- Onboarding structure:
  - [ ] Clarify for buddies what they should do (check-ins, context, safe space for questions).
  - [ ] Clarify what good looks like in ticket review (accuracy, process, tone, ownership).
  - [ ] Clarify what to focus on in shadow/reverse shadow sessions (workflows, troubleshooting, use of resources).
